Experience

Brilliant Detroit Research

I collaborated with students of various backgrounds to assist a non-profit. We organized their cluttered data with a program that reads from excel sheets and outputs to a database. Also, we provided recommendations including a systematic process of organizing future data, and methods to increase user input based on psychological aspects.

Design Jams

Design Jams are events where students work together and brainstorm solutions to a problem. These problems encourage user-centered thinking, collaboration, public speaking, and analysis. I attended various design jams including llamasoft, leasdership, and Airbnb.

Cryptocurrency Database

Our team created a program that predicts if news sentiment affects the price of cryptocurrency for the final project in our data-oriented programming class. This required us to parse data from three databases including a cryptocurrency API, an API of an ETF that represents cryptocurrency, and an API that finds the tone of the news. We then sorted the data using SQL and used Tableau to visualize our findings.

Client Website Redesign

Our team redesigned a website for the Ecologies Group at the University of Michigan. We interviewed the owner of the website and had to follow his guidelines as well as the accessibility requirements given by our professor. We ended up with an amazing website that won amongst our peers.

Leadership Series

The Leadership Series offers students an opportunity to build leadership skills including facilitation strategies, social identity development, and leadership as a professional. These events gave me a greater understanding of what is required to be a good leader while improving my public speaking and collaboration skills.

Consulting Case Studies

My Information Environments and Work class treated us as consultants and consisted of writing case studies. These case studies required us to do extensive research on a topic that had a problem we needed to solve. Our papers needed to be concise while providing the context and background, assessing main stakeholders, primary infrastructure, and requirements and solutions.
